Welcome to MSK!

Cambridge International Curriculum English Medium School

Masco School Kanchan is a modern, Cambridge Curriculum English Medium School (CAIE) located in a sprawling green campus at Kanchan, Rupganj. Offering Pre-school to Grade 8 education, the school is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced teachers, ensuring international standard education and mentoring. As a fresh option for quality education in the rural community, Masco School Kanchan plans to expand to classes 9 through A Levels in the next two years, providing a comfortable and stimulating environment for young learners.
